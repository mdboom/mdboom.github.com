Performance

In 2010, I was a performer in the Pulitzer prize-winning The Little Match Girl Passion by David Lang in Baltimore as part of the Evolution Music Series. It was rated as one of the top 10 classical music events in Baltimore in 2010 by the Baltimore Sun.

I was a member of the Peabody Renaissance Ensemble in Baltimore, MD for over 10 years.
